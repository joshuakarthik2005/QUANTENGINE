Built for quantitative workflows
Everything you need to sanity-check a distribution or risk profile in seconds.
Summary statistics
Mean, variance, standard deviation, skewness, kurtosis, quantiles and percentiles — computed with numerically stable algorithms.
Distribution fitting
Maximum-likelihood fits for Normal, Laplace and Student-t distributions, with clear parameter estimates and consistency checks.
Hypothesis tests
Jarque–Bera and Kolmogorov–Smirnov normality checks with p-values and explicit accept/reject labels for model assumptions.
Tail risk metrics
Historical VaR and CVaR at multiple confidence levels, plus a Hill tail index estimate to highlight heavy-tailed behaviour.
Time-Series Analysis
ACF, PACF, rolling statistics, volatility clustering, AR(1) modeling, and stationarity testing for temporal pattern detection.
Side-panel native
Runs in Chrome's side panel next to TradingView, Jupyter, PDFs or internal dashboards — no context switching required.
See it in context
QuantEngine is designed to sit alongside your existing research tools, not replace them.
Professional visualizations included:
  • Histogram with overlaid Normal, Laplace, and Student-t PDFs
  • Q–Q plots with toggle between Normal and Student-t reference distributions
  • ACF/PACF stem plots with confidence bands for time-series analysis
  • Rolling statistics charts tracking local mean and volatility
All charts are publication-ready with clean styling and proper axis labels.
Local-only by design
For firms that care deeply about privacy, latency and control.
